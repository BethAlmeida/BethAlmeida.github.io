AN ARTIST BOOK ~ A PERSONAL GEOGRAPHY

Where do you go when it gets dark? Although sometimes it's hard to see any hope, it's always there. When we do discover it, we enter a world entirely our own, embraced by light and love, somewhere inside of us that we can call home. Sometimes, knowing we have this home to go to, that we can access it always within us, is enough to remember what's important. This telescope is meant to be a beacon of hope, a symbol of home, that everyone can escape to when it gets dark. A home where constellations are made up of memories, and are constantly changing and remapping based off of your own present experience.
