Terra Flux Magazine

SEASONAL ISSUE ~ EARTH SCIENCE

Terra Flux Magazine is meant to facilitate a better understanding of the processes that have and continue to shape the Earth. It is a stand-still view of our ever-changing earth, particularly in moments of transition, and it is for anyone with an interest in science and the earth, of exploring the constant and beautiful changes that are always affecting our world. I hope Terra Flux will take you out of the present and on a much different scale, where you remember your roots and what really is important on this Earth, the beauty that is around us and affected by us from the beginning of time and after we’re gone.
